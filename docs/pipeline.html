Where everything meets

two sources of truth
Alpaca paper accountbroker of record

The ground truth for positions, orders, and fills. The loop writes orders to it; the listener hears fills from it; the ledger builder and dashboard read it back. When the self-log and the broker disagree, the broker wins.

trading_memory.db SQLite · WALmemory

trade_logs — the bot's self-log (entries, exits, feature payloads; feeds the cooldown gate). trades_reconciled — the broker-derived ledger for anything analytical. Clean views (trade_logs_clean, reconciled_closed…) de-noise both for offline work.

Model plumbing: every AI call runs Gemini 2.5 Flash with automatic fallback to a lighter model on quota errors, wrapped in exponential-backoff retry. Design stance: agents propose, gates dispose — position size, stop, and target are hardcoded outside the LLM context window, and every rejection lands in triage_history.csv so the funnel can be audited after the fact. Note: file names refer to the private codebase; published excerpts live in engineering/, and exact prompts, thresholds, and strategy parameters are not included.
